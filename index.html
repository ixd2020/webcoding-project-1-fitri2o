Who is Paul Rand?

Paul Rand - An art designer

Born Peretz Rosenbaum in 1914 and deceased in 1996, Paul Rand is a graphic design legend.
Throughout his 60-years long career, he changed America's opinion on visual communication.
With his editorial designs, advertisements,
and visual identity works, Rand brought avant-garde European ideas to the United-States,
mixing visual arts and commercial design.
His colourful combinations, approach of typography and use of media translate his desire
to "defamiliarize the ordinary".
His style consequently still have an impact on graphic design today.
